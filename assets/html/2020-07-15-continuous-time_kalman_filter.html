接下来按照离散kalman filter 的推导思路进行:

x^n+1=x~n+1+Δt∗Kt(yn+1−y~)=x~n+1+KT(Hxn+1+νn+1−Hx~n+1)=x~n+1+KTH(xn+1−x~n+1)+Kνn+1\begin{aligned} \hat{x}_{n+1} &= \tilde{x}_{n+1} + \Delta t*K_t(y_{n+1} - \tilde{y}) \\ &= \tilde{x}_{n+1} + K_T(Hx_{n+1} + \nu_{n+1} - H\tilde{x}_{n+1}) \\ &= \tilde{x}_{n+1} + K_TH(x_{n+1} - \tilde{x}_{n+1}) + K\nu_{n+1} \end{aligned} x^n+1​​=x~n+1​+Δt∗Kt​(yn+1​−y~​)=x~n+1​+KT​(Hxn+1​+νn+1​−Hx~n+1​)=x~n+1​+KT​H(xn+1​−x~n+1​)+Kνn+1​​

进而有

x^n+1−xn+1=x~n+1−xn+1+KTH(xn+1−x~n+1)+KTνn+1−e^n+1=−e~n+1+KTHe~n+1+KTνn+1e^n+1=(1−KTH)e~n+1−KTνt\begin{aligned} \hat{x}_{n+1} - x_{n+1} &= \tilde{x}_{n+1} - x_{n+1} + K_TH(x_{n+1} - \tilde{x}_{n+1}) + K_T\nu_{n+1} \\ -\hat{e}_{n+1} &= -\tilde{e}_{n+1} + K_TH\tilde{e}_{n+1} + K_T\nu_{n+1} \\ \hat{e}_{n+1} &= (1-K_TH)\tilde{e}_{n+1} - K_T\nu_t \\ \end{aligned} x^n+1​−xn+1​−e^n+1​e^n+1​​=x~n+1​−xn+1​+KT​H(xn+1​−x~n+1​)+KT​νn+1​=−e~n+1​+KT​He~n+1​+KT​νn+1​=(1−KT​H)e~n+1​−KT​νt​​

以上简单推导是离散 kalman filter 的内容, 可参考blog.

上一步, 我们得到了先验误差与后验误差的关系, 接下来我们通过求解一个KTK_TKT​, 使得在给定了先验误差的协方差矩阵Pn+1∣nP_{n+1|n}Pn+1∣n​下, 最小化后验误差的协方差矩阵Pn+1∣n+1P_{n+1|n+1}Pn+1∣n+1​.

接下来带入我们的定义, (注意, 下面推导的第一步等号右侧本该有四项, 我省略了两项, 因为那两项在下一步的取期望中为零)

e^n+1e^n+1T=(1−KTH)e~n+1e~n+1T(1−KTH)T−KTνn+1νn+1TKTT+...E[e^n+1e^n+1T]=E[(1−KTH)e~n+1e~n+1T(1−KTH)T−KTνn+1νn+1TKTT]Pn+1∣n+1∗Δt=(1−KTH)Pn+1∣n(1−KTH)T∗Δt−KTRKTTPn+1∣n+1=(1−KTH)Pn+1∣n(1−KTH)T−KT(RΔt)KTT(1)\begin{aligned} \hat{e}_{n+1}\hat{e}_{n+1}^T &= (1-K_TH)\tilde{e}_{n+1}\tilde{e}_{n+1}^T(1-K_TH)^T - K_T\nu_{n+1}\nu_{n+1}^TK_T^T + ... \\ E[\hat{e}_{n+1}\hat{e}_{n+1}^T] &= E[(1-K_TH)\tilde{e}_{n+1}\tilde{e}_{n+1}^T(1-K_TH)^T -K_T\nu_{n+1}\nu_{n+1}^TK_T^T] \\ P_{n+1|n+1}*\Delta t &= (1-K_TH)P_{n+1|n}(1-K_TH)^T * \Delta t - K_T R K_T^T \\ P_{n+1|n+1} &= (1-K_TH)P_{n+1|n}(1-K_TH)^T - K_T(\frac{R}{\Delta t})K_T^T \quad (1) \end{aligned} e^n+1​e^n+1T​E[e^n+1​e^n+1T​]Pn+1∣n+1​∗ΔtPn+1∣n+1​​=(1−KT​H)e~n+1​e~n+1T​(1−KT​H)T−KT​νn+1​νn+1T​KTT​+...=E[(1−KT​H)e~n+1​e~n+1T​(1−KT​H)T−KT​νn+1​νn+1T​KTT​]=(1−KT​H)Pn+1∣n​(1−KT​H)T∗Δt−KT​RKTT​=(1−KT​H)Pn+1∣n​(1−KT​H)T−KT​(ΔtR​)KTT​(1)​

通过极小化 Pn+1∣n+1P_{n+1|n+1}Pn+1∣n+1​ 来实现最优化估计 (原文中的优化目标是最小二乘误差, 即E(∥xn+1−x^n+1∥2)E(\parallel x_{n+1} - \hat{x}_{n+1} \parallel^2)E(∥xn+1​−x^n+1​∥2), 和极小化协方差矩阵的等效性没有证明, 我感觉是一样的, 原 kalman filter 是极小化协方差矩阵)

有令∂Pn+1∣n+1∂KT=0\frac{\partial{P_{n+1|n+1}}}{\partial{K_T}} = 0∂KT​∂Pn+1∣n+1​​=0, 并忽略二阶小量, 可以得到

KT=Pn+1∣nHT(HPn+1∣nHT+RΔt)−1=ΔtPn+1∣nHT(ΔtHPn+1∣nHT+R)−1≅ΔtPn+1∣nHTR−1\begin{aligned} K_T &= P_{n+1|n}H^T(HP_{n+1|n}H^T + \frac{R}{\Delta t})^{-1} \\ &= \Delta tP_{n+1|n}H^T(\Delta tHP_{n+1|n}H^T + R)^{-1} \\ &\cong \Delta tP_{n+1|n}H^TR^{-1} \end{aligned} KT​​=Pn+1∣n​HT(HPn+1∣n​HT+ΔtR​)−1=ΔtPn+1∣n​HT(ΔtHPn+1∣n​HT+R)−1≅ΔtPn+1∣n​HTR−1​

Kt=Pn+1∣nHTR−1K_t = P_{n+1|n}H^TR^{-1}Kt​=Pn+1∣n​HTR−1

带入KTK_TKT​到(1)中, 可以得到

$ P_{n+1|n+1} = (1-K_TH)P_{n+1|n} \quad (2)$

接下来我们建立先验误差和上一时刻的后验误差的联系

e~n+1=xn+1−xn+1~=xn+x˙∗Δt−xn^−x~˙n∗Δt=e^n+Δt∗(x˙n−x~˙n)=e^n+Δt∗(Axn+B(ut+ϵt)−Ax^n+But)=(1+ΔtA)e^n+ΔtBϵt\begin{aligned} \tilde{e}_{n+1} &= x_{n+1} - \tilde{x_{n+1}} \\ &= x_n + \dot{x}*\Delta t - \hat{x_n} - \dot{\tilde{x}}_n*\Delta t \\ &= \hat{e}_n + \Delta t*(\dot{x}_n - \dot{\tilde{x}}_n) \\ &= \hat{e}_n + \Delta t*(Ax_n + B(u_t + \epsilon_t) - A\hat{x}_n + Bu_t) \\ &= (1+\Delta tA)\hat{e}_n + \Delta tB\epsilon_t \\ \end{aligned} e~n+1​​=xn+1​−xn+1​~​=xn​+x˙∗Δt−xn​^​−x~˙n​∗Δt=e^n​+Δt∗(x˙n​−x~˙n​)=e^n​+Δt∗(Axn​+B(ut​+ϵt​)−Ax^n​+But​)=(1+ΔtA)e^n​+ΔtBϵt​​

进而构建先验误差协方差矩阵Pn+1∣nP_{n+1|n}Pn+1∣n​和上一时刻的后验误差协方差矩阵Pn∣nP_{n|n}Pn∣n​的联系, 自己推嗷.

Pn+1∣n=(1+ΔtA)Pn∣n(1+ΔtA)T+ΔtBQBT(3)P_{n+1|n} = (1+\Delta t A)P_{n|n}(1+\Delta t A)^T + \Delta tBQB^T \quad (3) Pn+1∣n​=(1+ΔtA)Pn∣n​(1+ΔtA)T+ΔtBQBT(3)

又因为我们有$ P_{n+1|n+1} = (1-K_TH)P_{n+1|n} = (1-\Delta t K_tH)P_{n+1|n}$

也就是$ P_{n|n} = (1-\Delta t K_{t-\Delta t}H)P_{n|n-1}$.

带入(3)中, 可以得到:

展开并忽略二阶小量得到(自己推嗷):

Pn+1∣n=Pn∣n−1+ΔtAPn∣n−1+ΔtPn∣n−1AT−ΔtKt−ΔtHPn∣n−1+ΔtBQBT(4)P_{n+1|n} = P_{n|n-1} + \Delta tAP_{n|n-1} + \Delta tP_{n|n-1}A^T - \Delta t K_{t-\Delta t}HP_{n|n-1} + \Delta tBQB^T \quad (4) Pn+1∣n​=Pn∣n−1​+ΔtAPn∣n−1​+ΔtPn∣n−1​AT−ΔtKt−Δt​HPn∣n−1​+ΔtBQBT(4)

接下来讨论一些性质:

  • Δt→0,KT≡Δt∗Kt→0\Delta t \to 0, K_T \equiv \Delta t*K_t \to 0Δt→0,KT​≡Δt∗Kt​→0
  • 由(2)得到, Δt→0,Pn+1∣n+1→Pn+1∣n\Delta t \to 0, P_{n+1|n+1} \to P_{n+1|n}Δt→0,Pn+1∣n+1​→Pn+1∣n​
  • 由(3)得到, Δt→0,Pn+1∣n→Pn∣n\Delta t \to 0, P_{n+1|n} \to P_{n|n}Δt→0,Pn+1∣n​→Pn∣n​
  • KT→0,Pn+1∣n+1→Pn∣nK_T \to 0, P_{n+1|n+1} \to P_{n|n}KT​→0,Pn+1∣n+1​→Pn∣n​

所以, 直观的可以感受到

P˙t=limΔt→0Pt+Δt−PtΔt=limΔt→0Pn+1∣n−Pn∣n−1Δt\begin{aligned} \dot{P}_t &= lim_{\Delta t \to 0}\frac{P_{t+\Delta t} - P_t}{\Delta t} \\ &= lim_{\Delta t \to 0} \frac{P_{n+1|n} - P_{n|n-1}}{\Delta t} \end{aligned} P˙t​​=limΔt→0​ΔtPt+Δt​−Pt​​=limΔt→0​ΔtPn+1∣n​−Pn∣n−1​​​

所以, 从(4)可以得到 (自己推嗷):

对于后验误差协方差稳定的情况, 即P˙t=0\dot{P}_t = 0P˙t​=0, 可以得到所谓的 Algebraic Riccati Equation :
